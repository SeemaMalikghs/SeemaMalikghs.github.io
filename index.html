My Teaching Journey
With over a decade of dedicated service in education, I have had the privilege of guiding hundreds of students through their academic journeys. My teaching approach blends traditional values with modern, innovative techniques to create an engaging classroom environment where every student feels seen, heard, and inspired.

Currently serving at Government High School Chak Bakhtu, I bring enthusiasm and dedication to every single class, ensuring that learning is never a burden — but always an adventure.

"Every child carries a unique spark. My role is not to fill a bucket, but to light a fire — and fan it with patience, empathy, and unwavering belief in their potential."

I believe that effective teaching blends empathy with structure, creativity with discipline, and passion with patience. Here are the core competencies I bring to my classroom every day:
